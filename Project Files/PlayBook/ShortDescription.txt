About:

Drop Swatch is a sophisticated, intuitive and multipurpose application used to cultivate and further advance the artistic endeavours of creative minds.

Explore unlimited hues and develop your color pallet with several color modes and texture options.

Instructions:

1.  Double tap canvas to drop new swatch.
2.  Drag, zoom and rotate swatches with common gestures.
3.  Single tap swatch to edit color and/or texture.
4.  Push swatches off screen to remove.
